Slide 2: Introduction to Cryptocurrency Exchanges

Cryptocurrency exchanges serve as the digital marketplaces where individuals and entities can buy, sell, and trade various cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many others. These exchanges play a pivotal role in the digital economy by acting as intermediaries, facilitating the exchange of digital assets. They are essential for anyone looking to enter the world of cryptocurrencies, making them a gateway to this innovative financial ecosystem.
